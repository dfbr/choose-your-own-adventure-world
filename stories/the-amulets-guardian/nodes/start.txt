You are a brave mermaid. The ocean is your home. But something is wrong. The water feels different. The fish whisper that the ocean is falling apart. They say the magical amulet is lost. Without it, the ocean will not survive. You decide to find the amulet. It is said to be hidden in a deep underwater cavern. You swim towards the cavern, feeling both excited and nervous. The entrance is dark and mysterious. Will you be able to bring the amulet back and save the ocean?
