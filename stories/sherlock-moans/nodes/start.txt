A thick London fog coils around the iron gates of Blackwood Manor. Sherlock Moans, his sharp eyes glinting beneath the brim of his hat, stands beside Dr. Watson. The manor looms, its windows flickering with candlelight, and a distant, mournful howl echoes through the night. Watson shivers. 'Are you certain about this, Sherlock?' he asks. Sherlock smiles, 'The game is afoot, Watson. Shall we enter the manor or investigate the grounds first?'
